# Week 2 — Data Exploration + Stats I

**Time:** ~14 hrs total (2 hrs/day × 7 days)
**Goal:** Learn to deeply understand a dataset before touching a model. Learn the statistics that explain what you're seeing.

## Schedule

| Day | Focus | Resource |
|-----|-------|----------|
| Mon | Pandas fundamentals — DataFrames, Series, indexing | `study-guide.md` §1 |
| Tue | Hands-on: load, inspect, slice a real dataset | `tutorial.ipynb` Part 1 |
| Wed | Stats I — mean, median, variance, standard deviation | `study-guide.md` §2 |
| Thu | Hands-on: compute and interpret stats on real data | `tutorial.ipynb` Part 2 |
| Fri | Distributions — normal, skewed, what they mean for ML | `study-guide.md` §3 |
| Sat | Hands-on: visualize distributions, spot problems | `tutorial.ipynb` Part 3 |
| Sun | Correlation — feature relationships and why they matter | `study-guide.md` §4 + `tutorial.ipynb` Part 4 |

## Deliverable

A full EDA (exploratory data analysis) report on a real dataset — distributions, missing values, outliers, and correlation heatmap — with written observations.

## What's Next

Week 3: Model evaluation. You'll use the statistical intuition from this week to understand precision, recall, and why accuracy alone is misleading.
